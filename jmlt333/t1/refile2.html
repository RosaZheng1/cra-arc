Change your return using certified tax software (ReFILE)

You may be looking for:

  • Change your return using a CRA secure portal

ReFILE is an online service that allows individuals and tax preparers send online adjustment requests for personal tax returns using certified tax software.

The ReFILE service is open for electronic filing from February 19, 2024 until January 24, 2025. It is available 21 hours a day. Due to maintenance activities, the service is not available from 3am to 6am (Eastern time).

Steps

  1. Wait for your tax return to be processed

    You must wait until you receive your notice of assessment (NOA) before you can request to make changes to your personal income tax return.

  2. Ensure you [used certified tax software]

    You or your tax preparer must have used certified tax software (NETFILE or EFILE) to file the return you are requesting to change.
