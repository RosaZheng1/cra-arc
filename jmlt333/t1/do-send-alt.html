Doing and sending your personal taxes

The Digital File My Return service is no longer available. To file your tax return, select one of the options listed below.

You have several ways to do your taxes, including options that are free or have varying costs, or others where you are invited by the CRA.

Steps

  1. Choose a method to fill out your tax return

    Electronically
    • Fast
    • Easy
    • Secure
    • Automated

    93% of tax returns are filed electroncially. There are 2 options.

    • Do it yourself using certified tax software

      Many software providers will prepare the tax return for free if you have a modest income or when the return is basic. Otherwise there is usually a small fee for the service.

      Full list of certified software options

    • Have it done by a third-party representative

      If someone else prepares your return, there may or may not be a cost. A representative can range from a trusted family member, to volunteers at a tax clinic, to tax professionals and accounting firms.
