Doing and sending your personal taxes

The Digital File My Return service is no longer available. To file your tax return, select one of the options listed below.

You have several ways to do your taxes, including options that are free or have varying costs, or others where you are invited by the CRA.

Steps

  1. Choose a method to fill out your tax return

    Electronically filing
    • Fast
    • Easy
    • Secure
    • Automated
    • How 93% of people file

    • Do it yourself using certified tax software

      Many software providers will prepare the tax return for free if you have a modest income or when the return is basic. Otherwise there is usually a small service fee.

      There are 3 options:

      Online
      Computer download
      Mobile app

    • Have it done by a third-party representative

      If someone else prepares your return, there may be a service fee.

      A representative can be:

      • A trusted friend or family member
      • A tax professional
      • A volunteer at a free tax clinic (for those with a modest income and a simple return)
    Paper filing

    Using a paper return will require you to:

    • Do several mathmatical calculations manually
    • Print out forms yourself or call the CRA and wait for them to be mailed to you
    • Wait up to 8 weeks for your return to be processed by the CRA

    To get the necessary paperwork, refer to: Get a T1 income tax package

    Invitation-only filing

    Individuals with a modest income and a simple return may receive an invite from CRA to participate in SimpleFile.

    The SimpleFile service allows eligible individuals to file their 2023 income tax and benefit return either by phone, digitally or by paper. These simplified methods of filing are free, secure, and easy to use. There are no complex forms to fill out or calculations to do. If you are eligible, you will receive an invitation letter from the CRA to use SimpleFile to file your 2023 income tax and benefit return.

    For full details, refer to: SimpleFile services
