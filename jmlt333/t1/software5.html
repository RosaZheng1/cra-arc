    Tax software companies are not representatives of the CRA.

    Keep informed that:

    • The CRA does not look at the privacy policies of software developers.

      It is your responsiblity to research the policies before buying or using tax software.

    • The use of the software is the responsibility of the software developer.

      The CRA cannot be held responsible if programming errors affect the calculation of amounts payable.

  3. Determine how you are using the software

    Most taxpayers are eligible to use NETFILE-certified software and the NETFILE service.

    • To fill in the return

      Review your software's restrictions before filling in the tax return.

      Refer to: Software restrictions and other information

    • To send the return

      Review the service's eligibility criteria before sending the tax return.

      Refer to: Using the NETFILE service
