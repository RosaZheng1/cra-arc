Getting an express notice after filing a personal tax return

"Express NOA" is an online service that allows individuals and authorized representatives to view the notice of assessment (NOA) or notice of reassessment (NOR) in certified tax software, right after the return has been received and processed by the CRA.

How it compares to an NOA or NOR

An express notice is a version of an NOA or NOR. The amount shown through the "Express NOA" service is the final amount that will also show on your NOA or NOR.

  • An express notice

    • Provides basic information
    • Available in tax software immediately after you file
    • Access one time only
    • Does not show your payment

  • An NOA or NOR

    • Provides a complete detailed summary
    • Available in your CRA secure portal or sent by mail after it is processed
    • Access anytime
    • May show your payment if received before it is issued

    Refer to: Notice of assessment (NOA)

How to use the "Express NOA" service

You are doing your own taxes

Steps

  1. Ensure you can access our online application

    You must have My Account for individuals.

  2. Verify you are using certified tax software

    You must be using NETFILE-certified tax software.

  3. Using the "Express NOA" service

    Once you file your return and receive your confirmation message, you will be prompted to enter your My Account user ID and password to use the "Express NOA" service.

    If you used Auto-fill my return

    If you have already entered your user ID and password and used the Auto-fill my return service, you may not need to re-enter them to access the Express NOA service if your login session remains active.

    The express NOA will be delivered into your software right after filing your return.

    You can save or print it. You will only be able to access your express notice one time.

    Tax returns that are filed online are generally assessed so quickly that any payments sent with a return will not show up on an Express NOA. You can confirm that we have received the payment for any amount owing on your tax return.

    Refer to: Confirm a payment to the CRA

You are doing someone else's taxes

Steps

  1. Ensure you can access our online applications

    You must be registered for Represent a Client (RAC) and have a RepID, GroupID, or Business Number (BN).

    The person you are doing taxes for must be registered for My Account for individuals.

  2. Complete the authorization process

    The person you are doing taxes for must authorize you for online access to their CRA tax information.

    Once you are authorized, you can receive an "Express NOA" for them.

    For more information, refer to: Authorize a representative

    If you are a professional service provider (such as an accountant or discounter)

    Your client must authorize you and complete Part E of Form T183, selecting the Express NOA option.

    Refer to: Form T183, Information Return for Electronic Filing of an Individual’s Income Tax and Benefit Return

  3. Verify you are using certified tax software

    • If you are a family member or friend

      You must be using NETFILE-certified tax software

    • If you are a professional service provider

      You must be a certified electronic filer and using EFILE certified tax software.

      Refer to: EFILE for electronic filers

  4. Using the "Express NOA" service

    Once you file your return and receive your confirmation message, you will be prompted to enter your RAC user ID and password to use the "Express NOA" service.

    If you used Auto-fill my return

    If you have already entered your user ID and password and used the Auto-fill my return service, you may not need to re-enter them to access the Express NOA service if your login session remains active.

    The express NOA will be delivered into your software right after filing the return.

    You can save or print it. You will only be able to access the express notice one time.

    Tax returns that are filed online are generally assessed so quickly that any payments sent with a return will not show up on an Express NOA. You can confirm that we have received the payment for any amount owing on your tax return.

    Refer to: Confirm a payment to the CRA
