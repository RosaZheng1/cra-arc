Get a proof of income statement

A proof of income statement is a simple version of your tax assessment that summarizes your income and deductions for a specific year.

You might need to provide proof of income statement to a bank, a government, or others when you apply for:

  • A loan
  • To qualify for a benefit or subsidy
  • Other reasons that require proof of finances

The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) can provide you with this document.

How to get one

Online
Sign in to My Account Registerfor CRA My Account
  • Go to the left menu and select “Tax returns”
  • Go to the column on the right, scroll down and select “Underused housing tax”
  • Select the “File a UHT return” button
By mail

Call the CRA to ask for your proof of income statement to be mailed to you.

Order a copy by phone

Call our automated line to get a proof of income statement mailed to you.

Before you call

To verify your identity, you'll need

  • Social Insurance Number
  • Full name and date of birth
  • Your complete address
  • Line 15000 from your most recent assessment

If you are calling the CRA on behalf of someone else, you must be an authorized representative
