Get a proof of income statement

A proof of income statement is a simple version of your tax assessment that summarizes your income and deductions for a specific year.

The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) can provide you with this document.

You might need to provide proof of income statement to a bank, a government, or others when you apply for:

  • A loan
  • To qualify for a benefit or subsidy
  • Other reasons that require proof of finances
