Using an automated service to fill in your tax return

Auto-fill my return is an online service that allows individuals and authorized representatives using certified tax software, to automatically fill in parts of a personal tax return with information that the CRA has available at the time of the request.

How to use this service

You or your tax preparer can use Auto-fill my return.

You are doing your own taxes

To use the Auto-fill my return service, you must:

  • Be registered for CRA's My Account

    Refer to: My Account for individuals

  • Use a certified tax software

    Refer to: Certified tax software (NETFILE)

When you are ready to use Auto-fill my return, your software will prompt you to enter your My Account user ID and password. Follow the steps provided in the software you are using. Once Auto-fill my return has populated your return with the information, make sure that all the proper fields on the return are filled in and that the information provided is true, accurate and complete before you file your return.

You are doing someone else's taxes

You can use the Auto-fill my return service:

If you are a family member or friend

To use the Auto-fill my return service for a family member or friend, you must:

  • Have them register for My Account

    Refer to: My Account for individuals

  • Be registered in Represent a Client

    Get your RepID

    Refer to: Represent a Client

  • Have them authorize you

    Give them your RepID so they can authorize you as their representative

    Refer to: Authorize a representative

You must also use a certified tax software (NETFILE).

Once you are authorized, you can use Auto-fill my return to access their tax information and file a return for them. Each individual should enter their own CRA user ID and password to use this service.

If you are a professional service provider (such as an accountant or discounter)

To use the Auto-fill my return service, you must:

  • Be a certified electronic filer

    Refer to: Apply for EFILE

  • Be registered in Represent a Client

    Have a RepID, GroupID, or Business Number (BN)

    Refer to: Represent a Client

  • Use a certified tax software

    Refer to: Certified tax software (EFILE)

Your client must authorize you

Your client must authorize you for online access to their CRA tax information using one of the following methods:

  • Directly in their CRA My Account
  • Through your EFILE certified software
    • The signature page generated by the software is to be signed by the client and kept by you for 6 years
  • By confirming your request within CRA secure portals
    • Confirming in their My Account the authorization request you submitted in Represent a Client

Once you are authorized, you can use Auto-fill my return to access their tax information and file a return for them. Each individual should enter their own CRA user ID and password to use this service.

Your responsibilities when using this service

Before you file a tax return with the CRA using the information delivered by Auto-fill my return you have to ensure that all proper fields on the return are filled in correctly, including information not delivered by Auto-fill my return, and that the information provided is true, accurate and complete. It remains the taxpayer's responsibility to report accurately their income before a tax return is submitted to the CRA.

It is your responsibility to contact the issuer for any missing or incorrect tax slips.

You can contact the CRA if you:

  • Notice a mistake in the tax-related information
  • Have an account-specific question about other tax-related information
  • Are having problems with the service's login process

Information delivered by this service

The Auto-fill my return service will deliver all of the information listed below that is available at the time of the request. The CRA receives tax information from third parties and as such, the CRA should have most tax information slips and other tax-related information by mid-March of each year. However, if the information is not available, it is possible that the CRA has not yet received it. If that is the case, please try the service again later.

If a slip was issued to you with an error in your Social Insurance Number (SIN), first name, or last name, it will not be available on My Account or Auto-fill my return. Contact the issuer for any missing or incorrect slips.

T4A slips may include additional amounts

T4A slips available in Auto-fill my return may include amounts received for the following COVID-19 Emergency Relief measures:

  • Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB)
  • Canada Emergency Student Benefit (CESB)
  • Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB)
  • Canada Recovery Sickness Benefit (CRSB)
  • Canada Recovery Caregiving Benefit (CRCB)
  • Canada Worker Lockdown Benefit (CWLB)
  • Provincial/Territorial COVID-19 Financial assistance payments
Availability of RRSP and PRPP receipts

Receipts for RRSP and PRPP contributions made in the first 60 days of the 2024 year may not be available until May 2024.
