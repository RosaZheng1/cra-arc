Using an automated service to fill in your tax return

Auto-fill my return is an online service that allows individuals and authorized representatives using certified tax software, to automatically fill in parts of a personal tax return with information that the CRA has available at the time of the request.

How to use this service

You or your tax preparer can use Auto-fill my return.

You are doing your own taxes

To use the Auto-fill my return service, you must:

  • Be registered for CRA's My Account

    Refer to: CRA My Account

  • Use a certified tax software

    Refer to: Certified tax software (NETFILE)

When you are ready to use Auto-fill my return, your software will prompt you to enter your My Account user ID and password. Follow the steps provided in the software you are using. Once Auto-fill my return has populated your return with the information, make sure that all the proper fields on the return are filled in and that the information provided is true, accurate and complete before you file your return.

You are doing someone else's taxes
If you are a family member or friend
If you are a professional service provider (such as an accountant or discounter)

To use the Auto-fill my return service, you must:

  • Be a certified electronic filer

    Refer to: Apply for EFILE

  • Be registered in Represent a Client

    Refer to: Represent a Client

  • Use a certified tax software

    Refer to: Certified tax software (EFILE)

Your client must:

Authorize you for online access

Your client must authorize you for online access to their CRA tax information using one of the following methods:

  • Directly in their CRA My Account
  • Through your EFILE certified software
    • The signature page generated by the software is to be signed by the client and kept by you for 6 years
  • By confirming your request within CRA secure portals
    • Confirming in their My Account the authorization request you submitted in Represent a Client
