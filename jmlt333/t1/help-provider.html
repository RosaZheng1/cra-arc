Using a professional service provider to file a personal income tax return

You may be looking for:

  • Filing as a professional service provider

You can take your tax documents to a professional service provider, who for a fee, will file your tax return for you.

A professional service provider can be:

  • A firm
  • An organization
  • An individual

A professional service provider can also be a discounter. A discounter is a tax preparer who calculates your refund, and immediatley pays you part of it before filing your tax return.

For details, refer to: Discounter information

Almost all Canadians are eligible to have their tax return filed electronically. Your service provider will confirm based on your situation.
