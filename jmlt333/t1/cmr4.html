Change your return using a CRA secure portal

You may be looking for:

  • Change your return using certified tax software (ReFILE)

Change my return is a feature in My Account (or Represent a Client) that allows you to send online adjustment requests for your personal tax return.

You are not able to use Change my return to apply for benefits or credits.

Disability tax credit (DTC)

Once you have applied and been approved to claim the disability tax credit (DTC), the option to make changes related to it will become available.

For more information about applying for this credit, refer to Disability tax credit (DTC)

The Change my return feature includes questions and messages when the CRA:

  • Needs more information
  • Detects errors
  • Needs supporting documents to finalize your request

Steps

  1. Wait for your tax return to be processed

    You must wait until you receive your notice of assessment (NOA) before you can request to make changes to your personal income tax return.

  2. Ensure you can access our online applications

    • If you are an individual changing your own tax return

      You must have a CRA My Account

      Refer to: My Account for individuals

    • If you are a tax preparer changing someone else's tax return

      You must be registered for Represent a Client (RAC) and have at least level 2 access

      Refer to: Represent a Client

  3. Determine if you can use this feature

    You must meet all 3 criteria below to use Change my return:

    If you do not meet all of the above criteria, you can only request to make your changes by mail.

    Based on your selections above:

    You can use this feature

    You are able to use the Change my return feature within your CRA secure portal.

    Sign in to your CRA secure portal
    • Go to the left menu and select “Tax returns”
    • Go to the column on the right, and select “Change my return”

    • If your request for changes is approved

      We will send you a notice of reassessment showing any changes to yoru return, as well as any refund

    • If your request for changes is denied

      We will send you a letter explaining why we did not make changes you asked for, or why no changes were needed

Heading

  • View an option based on your situation

    The option to claim the disability tax credit (DTC) may be available.

    Claiming the DTC

    Once you have applied and been approved to claim the disability tax credit (DTC), the option to make changes related to it will become available.

    If not, this option will not be shown.

    After approval, you can claim the DTC for:

    • Yourself
    • Your dependant(s)
    • Your spouse or common-law partner

    For more information about the DTC, or how to apply, refer to Disability tax credit (DTC)

  • Select a tax year

    Select the year you want to change from the list of available years in the drop-down menu.

    When you have made the changes for one year, you can then make changes to another year.

  • Change my return

    You will have an option to change a line or add amounts from tax slips.

    If you are making an election to split pension income

    We will only process your request if both the transferring spouse or common-law partner and the receiving spouse or common-law partner asked for an adjustment to the elected split-pension amount.

    For more information, refer to: How to split your pension income
