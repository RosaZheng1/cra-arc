Deliver

Before launching the final version of the new web pages, the following is required:

  • SMEs verify the accuracy of content changes and give approval Web Development team:
    • codes the approved prototype in HTML
    • update or re-direct any links, if the re-design as required
    • ensure all other Canada.ca pages are updated or re-directed, accordingly
  • project team will perform quality assurance review:
    • examine all aspects of the pages and ensure there are no errors or broken links
    • proofread the copy and content
    • confirm that font size and style, and element styles are consistent with the Web Experience Toolkit (WET) guidelines
    • ensure that forms or calculators (if required) function as planned
  • once the English versions of the pages are approved, they’re translated into French.
