Creating a user centred design - CRA web service manual

The CRA’s User Centred Design process builds a deep sense of empathy essential for designing communication products and applications tailored to meet both the needs of Canadians and the CRA.

Connecting the right people with our User Experience Researchers and Designers to test prototypes, provides evidence ensuring that they can quickly find the information, easily understand the content, and successfully complete their tasks.

Sections

Plan
Kickoff off project; determine scope, timelines & KPIs
Discover
Analysis of pre-existing data; perform baseline test and task scenario workshop
Design
Design, test and iterate based on evidence gathered
Deliver
Code, translate, perform quality assurance review then launch
Monitor and refine
Post-launch monitoring of delivered content
