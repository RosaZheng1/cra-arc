Sections

Principle of choices
Create pages that offer meaningful choices to users and keep the choices focused on a particular task
Principle of disclosure
Show only enough information to help the user understand what kind of information they’ll find as they dig deeper
Principle of front doors
Design content by assuming that at least half of the website’s visitors arrive at a page through a path other than what you expected
Principle of objects
Treat content as a living, breathing thing with a lifecycle, behaviours, and attributes
Principle of growth
Assume the content you have today is a small fraction of the content you will have tomorrow
Principle of exemplars
Show examples of the content in action, where possible
Principle of multiple classification
Offer users several different classification schemes to browse the site’s content
Principle of focused navigation
Keep navigation menus simple
