Section with a conditional top bar

Appearance

A bar will be displayed at the top of the screen when this section is fully out of the viewport (with class="show-none").

View state: all

HTML code 
<section class="wb-inview show-none" data-inview="top-bar">	
  <p>Your content...</p>
</section>

<!-- Conditional top bar -->
<section id="top-bar" class="wb-overlay modal-content overlay-def wb-bar-t">
  <header>
    <h2 class="modal-title">Example of a top bar</h2>
  </header>
  <ul class="list-inline">
    <li><button class="btn btn-default" type="button">Button 1</button></li>
    <li><button class="btn btn-default" type="button">Button 2</button></li>
    <li><button class="btn btn-default" type="button">Button 3</button></li>
  </ul>
</section>

Fade in or out effect for the next section

Appearance

The next section will fade in when this section is partially out of the viewport.

View state: all

Section that fades in/out

Your content...

HTML code 
<section class="wb-inview" data-inview="fade-effect">	
<p>The next section will fade in...</p>
<p><strong>View state:</strong> <code class="view-state-status">all</code></p>
</section>

<!-- Fade in/out section -->
<section id="fade-effect" class="fade">
<div class="well">
<h2>Section that fades in/out</h2>
<p>Your content...</p>
</div>
</section>
