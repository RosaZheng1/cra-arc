  1. Determine when the benefit must be included in your employee’s income

    Under section 7 of the ITA, the timing of when a security options taxable benefit must be included in your employee’s income depends on whether you are a CCPC  or not.

  2. Calculate the taxable benefit and your employee’s inclusion in income

    To calculate the taxable benefit that must be included in your employee’s income, use the following formula:

    Taxable Benefit = (FMV - Option Price) x Number of Shares

    Where:

    • FMV is the fair market value of the shares at the time the option is exercised.
    • Option Price is the price paid for the shares.
    • Number of Shares is the number of shares purchased.

  3. Reporting the benefit to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA)

    Report the taxable benefit on your employee’s T4 slip under the “Other income” section. Ensure you include the amount and any relevant details required by the CRA.
