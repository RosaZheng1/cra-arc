<details><summary>What is a SCDE</summary>
<div id="ajax-scde2">
<p>A residence is considered a self-contained domestic establishment (SCDE) if the following apply: </p>
<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-and">
  <li class="cnjnctn-col">A house, apartment, or other similar place of residence where a person usually sleeps and eats.</li>
  <li class="cnjnctn-col">A living unit with restricted access that contains a kitchen, bathroom, and sleeping facilities</li>
  <li class="cnjnctn-col">The SCDE must be separate from any other living unit in the same building.</li>
</ul>
<p class="mrgn-bttm-md"><span class="fas fa-times text-danger fa-lg"></span> Generally, a room in a hotel, dormitory, boarding house, or bunkhouse is not considered a SCDE.  </p> </div>
