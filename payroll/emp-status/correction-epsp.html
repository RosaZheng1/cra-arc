Corriger vos erreurs de déclaration

If you believe you may be carrying on a PSB and would like to correct the tax returns you previously filed, you can contact the CRA’s Voluntary Disclosure Program to determine if you meet the eligibility requirements and submit an application.

Learn more: Voluntary Disclosures Program – Introduction

Change your T2 tax returns

If you would like to change your T2 return, you can request a reassessment.

Learn more: Reassessments – Adjustments to your T2 return

T4 and T4A slips

If you were previously an employee and then formed a corporation, and a new contract (not an employment contract) is made with your corporation mid way through the year, the payer (employer) will provide you with a T4 slip for employment related purposes and a T4A slip to your corporation for the payments they made to it.

Learn more: Fill out the slips and summaries
