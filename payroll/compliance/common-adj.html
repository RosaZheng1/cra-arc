Common amendments following an examination

Corrections to an information return that has already been filed are completed through the amendment process.

When is an amendment processed

An amendment is processed to:

  • Change, delete or add information to your payroll information return
  • Provide correct information to Employment and Social Development Canada's (ESDC) for the record of earnings (ROE) file
  • Provide correct information for the T1 return matching programs
  • Provide a correct slip to the individual whose record is being changed

When an amendment is required

An amendment may be required for the following:

  • Correct a reporting error you made
  • Correct a processing error made by the CRA
  • Correct CPP and EI discrepancy
  • Result of a CPP/EI Ruling decision
  • Result of a change to the record of earnings (ROE)
  • Result of a request from Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC)

What are the information return types

Amendments to the following information returns may be processed:

  • T4
  • T4A
  • T4A-NR
  • T4A(OAS)
  • T4A-RCA
  • T4RIF
  • T4RSP
  • T4A(P)
  • T4E
  • T1204
