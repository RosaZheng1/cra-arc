Claim eligible deductions to reduce taxable income

Deductions are certain expenses and other amounts that you subtract from your total income or your net income when you fill out your income tax and benefit return. Because the taxes you owe are calculated using your income, deductions reduce the amount of tax that you may have to pay. Some deductions reduce your net income and others reduce your taxable income.

  • Total income
  • −minus Certain types of deductions
  • =equals Net income
  • −minus Other types of deductions
  • =equals Taxable income (used to calculate how much tax you owe)

Your taxable income is used to calculate how much federal and provincial or territorial tax you may have to pay. You can reduce your taxable income by claiming deductions.
