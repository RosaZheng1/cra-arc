Employment status: Employee or self-employed

It is important to determine whether a worker is:

  • An employee
  • A self-employed individual

This relationship directly affects an individual's entitlement to employment benefits, pension plans, and more.

Additionally, it affects how an individual and business meets their obligations and how they report to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).
